Dashcameras (2 & 3 Channel)

We specialize in 2-Channel (Front & Rear) and 3-Channel (Front, Rear & Interior) setups. We hardwire every unit directly to your fuse box, enabling 24/7 parking mode to record hits while you are away, all while keeping your cigarette lighter socket free and wires completely hidden.

A hidden remote GPS and killswitch system bridges the gap between ownership and true control, ensuring that your vehicle remains exactly where it belongs even when you aren't behind the wheel. It serves as the ultimate failsafe, providing the immediate power to track and disable your car the moment its security is compromised.
